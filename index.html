Villa Allure & Villa Ahurewa

Identical in design, each villa features two master bedrooms with expansive beds, ensuite bathrooms, an open plan kitchen/living space, a private pool, a spacious outdoor lounge, and sun loungers. The enclosed lush gardens and premium furnishings ensure both luxury and privacy.

Our sleek and completely private villas are more than a getaway; they’re a sanctuary. "Allure" evokes mystery and attraction, while "Ahurewa" signifies a sacred place. These names reflect the villas' undeniable ambiance.

Tucked away from the chaos yet just a few minutes from the coast, our twin contemporary villas sit in Ahangama, Sri Lanka’s rising hotspot. Ideally located between Galle and Matara, they offer both seclusion and easy access to world-class beaches, surf breaks, and vibrant surroundings.
